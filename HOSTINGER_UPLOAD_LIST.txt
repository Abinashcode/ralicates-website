VERIFY PATHS IN HTML:
- CSS: href="style.css" (not styles/style.css)
- CSS: href="about.css" (not styles/about.css)
- JS: src="script.js" (not scripts/script.js)
- Logo: src="assets/logo.svg" (this is correct)
